Chocolate Whey Too Many Gains

Description

This frosty, protein-filled shake is sure to satisfy your muscles and taste buds. It’s the ideal post-workout meal but can be enjoyed as a rich dessert all the same.

Warning: You will never want to go to Shake Shack ever again!

Ingredients

  • 2% Milk
  • 0% Fat Greek Yogurt
  • Natural Peanut Butter
  • Ripe Banana
  • Chocolate Whey Protein Powder
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Add 4oz milk into blender
  2. Add 1/2 cup greek yogurt into milk
  3. With your favourite spoon, add one spoonful peanut butter into the wet mix (I won’t tell anyone how much you use
  4. Insert half banana. Put the other half into the wet mix
  5. Add two scoops whey protein
  6. Add 7 ice cubes
  7. Blend for 30 seconds

Voila! Upon drinking this, you will look and feel like The Incredible Hulk.
